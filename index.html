Physalis Seed Information

Seed Storage

Storing Date: 15 June 2025

Source: From ripe fruit bought from Lidl, Linz

How to Plant Physalis Seeds

  • Sowing Depth: 0.5 cm
  • Spacing: 60 cm between plants
  • Indoor/Outdoor: Start indoors 6–8 weeks before last frost; transplant outdoors after frost
  • Water Needs: Keep soil consistently moist, but not soggy
  • Soil Mix: Light, well-draining soil (pH 5.5–7.0)
  • Temperature for Germination: 20–25°C
  • Humidity: Moderate (40–60%)
  • Sunlight: Full sun (at least 6 hours/day)

Best Areas in Pakistan for Physalis

Physalis grows well in cooler, moderate climates. Suitable regions in Pakistan include:

  • Islamabad
  • Murree
  • Abbottabad
  • Swat
  • Quetta
  • Northern Punjab (moderate summer, not frost-prone)

Avoid extreme heat or frost-prone zones like interior Sindh during peak summer.
